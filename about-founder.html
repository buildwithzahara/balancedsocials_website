My Story

Why I built Balanced Socials

"Social media should work for your business, not the other way around."

My path to Balanced Socials wasn't a straight line, and I think that's exactly what makes me better at this work. I've been a small business owner. I've worked in corporate administration. I spent years as a freelance social media manager and marketer before taking a step back to accept a hybrid accounts and marketing role at a growing small business.

That last role was a turning point. Working inside a business doing over six figures a year, I got to see how marketing actually connects to revenue, not just follower counts, but real business growth. I learned to think like a business owner, not just a content creator. And that experience gave me the confidence to rebrand and go all in on Balanced Socials.

Now I work with service-based businesses: beauty clinics (injectables included), studios, coaches and consultants, helping them build an Instagram presence that's genuinely theirs and genuinely effective.

I specialise in Instagram-first strategy because I believe in doing one thing exceptionally well before spreading thin. Most of my clients have been invisible on Instagram despite having brilliant businesses. That changes when we work together.

Zahara, Founder of Balanced Socials

How I Think

I build brands that feel like something.

Most social media looks fine. Fine isn't the goal. I'm interested in building a presence that feels distinctly yours, that your ideal client sees and immediately knows it's for them.

That requires more than content creation. It requires understanding your business deeply, your clients, your language, the energy you bring to your work. I do that research before anything else, because the quality of the strategy determines everything that follows.

Strategy First

I start with your business goals, not a content calendar. Everything flows from a clear strategy built for you.

Your Voice, Always

I invest real time understanding how you communicate. Your audience will never know I'm behind the scenes.

Depth Over Spread

I specialise in Instagram because doing one thing brilliantly beats doing five things averagely. Always.

Real Partnership

I'm not a vendor. I genuinely care about your success, and I'll tell you what isn't working as much as what is.
