Step by Step

From first conversation to ongoing results

1
Getting Started

Initial Enquiry

⏱ 30–45 minutes · Free

We start with your enquiry, not a pitch. We'll ask about your business, your goals, your current social media challenges, and what you've already tried. We'll be honest about whether we think we can help, and what that help would look like.

By the end of our initial exchange, you'll have a clearer picture of your situation even if we don't end up working together.

No obligation Honest advice Clear next steps
2
Onboarding

Brand Deep Dive

⏱ Week 1

If we decide to move forward, we go deep into your brand. We review your existing content, your competitors, your audience, and, most importantly, the way you communicate. We're building a complete picture before we create a single piece of content.

Brand voice audit Competitor analysis Audience profile
3
Strategy

Content Strategy Build

⏱ Week 2

We build a custom content strategy, your content pillars, posting frequency, platform priorities, content formats, and a 30-day content plan. You review and approve before anything goes live. Nothing is templated; everything is built specifically for your business.

Content pillars 30-day plan Platform strategy Client approval
4
Execution

Content Creation & Publishing

⏱ Ongoing

We create, schedule, and publish your content across your platforms, maintaining your brand voice consistently. You'll see content before it goes live and can request changes. We handle community management, hashtag strategy, and all the details you don't want to think about.

Content creation Scheduling Community management Preview & approval
5
Review

Monthly Reporting & Optimisation

⏱ Every month

At the end of each month, we review what worked, what didn't, and why. You get a clear report with the metrics that matter, not vanity numbers, but reach, engagement, leads, and progress toward your goals. We use this to refine the strategy for the next month.

Monthly report Strategy review Goal tracking
What I Use

Platforms & tools I work with

I don't want to just rely on tools that do the job. I use the right ones strategically, because the goal isn't to produce content. It's to produce excellent content that actually moves your business forward.

Instagram

My primary platform. Feed posts, Reels, Stories, and a strategy built around how the algorithm actually works right now.

Canva

On-brand visual design for graphics, carousels, and story templates that look custom, not cookie-cutter.

Adobe Creative Suite

For more refined design work, video editing, and creative assets that need a level of polish beyond the basics.

Rella

Content planning and scheduling built specifically for Instagram-first workflows. Clean, visual, and genuinely useful.

Custom-Coded Tools

Where necessary, I build and integrate bespoke solutions specific to each client's workflow. No sloppy workarounds, no unnecessary complexity.

Expanding Marketing Knowledge

What You Can Expect

The practical details

Communication

You'll have a direct line to me. I respond within 24 hours on business days, almost always faster.

Content Approval

Nothing goes live without your sign-off. I batch content a week ahead so you're never rushed. Your brand, your final say, always.

Timeline to Results

Better consistency and content quality from the first week. Engagement improvements within 1 to 3 months. I'll always be straight with you about what's realistic — results take time, and I'd rather set honest expectations than overpromise.

Contract & Flexibility

Management clients start on a 3-month minimum, because good strategy needs time. After that, rolling monthly with 30 days notice. Consulting and strategy sessions are one-off, no minimum.
